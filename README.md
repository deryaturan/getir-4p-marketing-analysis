# GETIR – 4P Marketing Mix Case Study

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the 4P Marketing Mix (Product, Price, Place, Promotion) of the Getir brand and evaluates survey data using chi-square statistical analysis.

The study examines customer perceptions of Getir services and how demographic factors influence preferences.

---

## Marketing Analysis (4P)

### Product
Getir provides fast delivery services including groceries, food delivery, and water delivery. These are classified as convenience products.

### Price
Pricing strategies vary:
- Grocery and water services: cost-based pricing
- Food delivery (GetirYemek): competition-based pricing

### Place (Distribution)
Getir uses a direct distribution model through its mobile application and courier system. It operates extensively across Turkey and abroad.

### Promotion
Getir uses a pull strategy with:
- Social media advertising
- TV commercials
- Coupons and promotional campaigns

---

## Survey Analysis

A survey of 28 participants was conducted and analyzed using SPSS chi-square tests.

---

## Hypothesis Results

### 1. Gender vs Design Perception
No significant relationship found (H0 supported).

### 2. Education vs Pricing Perception
Significant relationship found (H1 supported).

### 3. Age vs Accessibility Perception
Significant relationship found (H1 supported).

### 4. Field of Study vs Promotion Effectiveness
No significant relationship found (H0 supported).

---

## Conclusion
The study shows that demographic variables such as education and age can influence perceptions of Getir’s marketing strategies, while gender and field of study have no significant effect.
